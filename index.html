üåê How DisasterConnect Works

DisasterConnect is not just a list of agencies. It‚Äôs an AI-powered network analysis and mapping platform inspired by tools like Kumu and modern GIS systems. Instead of forcing people to sift through dozens of websites and PDFs, DisasterConnect builds a single interactive map revealing:

  • Which agencies are responsible for specific disaster services
  • How different agencies connect and coordinate
  • Where critical resources like shelters and cooling centers are located

It bridges the gap between complex government data and the real-life questions people ask during emergencies: Who can help me? How do I reach them? What resources are available in my neighborhood?
